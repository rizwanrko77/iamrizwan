I am

Father, cognizant, wonderer, &
a founder.

I'm Rizwan — I build XApproach, a white-label AI video knowledge base that helps teams and educators turn long-form content into searchable, actionable intelligence. The goal is to keep education open and empower educators to monetize their time.

Before XApproach, I built and sold The Networker (community platform, successful exit), founded Humgrow (B2B SaaS — shut down after 6 months, learned hard lessons), and currently do product spec work at Meetly — competitive research, feature scoping, the unglamorous stuff that actually ships products.

I work with AI every day — not as a buzzword, but as a co-pilot. I vibe-code, ship fast, and build with zero-infrastructure budgets. Most of my stack runs on free tiers because constraints make you creative.

I believe in building real things that solve real problems — not decks for investors. I dropped out of university to build. I write about everything I learn, including the failures. Especially the failures.

Bio

Welcome to the long version.

I'm Mohd Rizwan — a builder-founder from India who ships AI-powered products with a high bias for action and a near-zero infrastructure budget.

I'm currently building XApproach, a white-label AI video knowledge base for teams and educators. I also do product spec work at Meetly — competitive research, feature scoping, and the strategic groundwork behind shipping features.

Before this, I built and sold The Networker (a community platform — successful exit), founded and honestly shut down Humgrow (a B2B SaaS that taught me everything about what not to do), and have been coding with AI as my primary co-pilot since before it was cool to say that.

The beginning. I left university because I couldn't sit through another lecture about theory when I wanted to build things. Not a dramatic Silicon Valley dropout story — just a practical decision. I could learn faster by doing.

The Networker (2021–2023). My first real product. A community platform built entirely with no-code tools. It found its audience, grew organically, and I eventually sold it. The exit wasn't life-changing money, but it proved to me that I could build something people would pay for — and that someone else would pay to own.

Humgrow (2023). Riding the high from The Networker, I built Humgrow — a B2B SaaS for growth teams. I spent ₹14L building the wrong MVP, ignored every sign that the market didn't want what I was making, and shut it down after 6 months. It was the most expensive education I've ever paid for, and worth every rupee.

XApproach (2024–present). What I'm building now. A white-label AI video knowledge base — teams and educators upload long-form video content, and XApproach makes it searchable, clippable, and actionable. The mission: keep education open and empower educators to monetize their time. Built with Fastify, PostgreSQL, Cloudflare Workers, and a lot of AI-assisted coding.

Contract work: Meetly. In parallel, I do product spec work for Meetly (a video SaaS startup). Competitive analysis, feature scoping, product strategy — the unglamorous work that makes shipping possible.

How I work. I vibe-code. I use AI as a co-pilot for everything — not to replace thinking, but to accelerate it. My stack runs on free tiers wherever possible. I build in public because I believe transparency is a competitive advantage, not a vulnerability.
